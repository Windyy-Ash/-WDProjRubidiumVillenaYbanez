Ingredients || How to get it

Mint - Found in the wild throughout Teyvat, especially Mondstadt and Liyue. Can also be grown in the Serenitea Pot.
Apple - Found in the wild, dropped from trees, and sometimes sold by Bolai in Liyue Harbor.
Cheese - Processed from Milk (3x) or bought from Sara at Good Hunter in Mondstadt.
Sunsettia - Found on trees throughout Teyvat, especially in Mondstadt and Liyue.
Sweet Flower - Found in the wild, used in many recipes. Can be grown in the Serenitea Pot.
Bird Egg - Found in nests on top of trees, cliffs, and rooftops.
Fowl - Dropped by wild birds and can be bought from some vendors.
Fish - Caught by fishing or bought from vendors in Liyue and Inazuma.
Rice - Purchased from general goods stores in Liyue and Inazuma.
Potato - Purchased from general goods stores in Mondstadt and Liyue.
Raw Meat - Dropped by wild boars and foxes, or bought from Draff in Springvale.
Crab - Found near water bodies, especially in Liyue and Inazuma.
Tofu - Purchased from general goods stores in Liyue.
Mushroom - Found at the base of trees and buildings, especially in Mondstadt.
