Mondstadt Prologue

The Mondstadt Prologue Archon Quest follows the Traveler’s arrival in the city of freedom and wind while searching for their lost sibling. After meeting Amber and joining the Knights of Favonius, the Traveler helps defend the city from attacks by the dragon Stormterror. With Jean, Kaeya, Diluc, and Venti, the Traveler restores balance to corrupted temples across Mondstadt.

The truth is revealed when Stormterror is identified as Dvalin, an old companion of the Anemo Archon manipulated by the Abyss Order. With Venti’s help, Dvalin is purified and Mondstadt is saved. However, Signora soon appears and steals Venti’s Gnosis, and the Traveler sets their sights on Liyue.
