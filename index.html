Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG set on the magical continent of Teyvat. You play as the Traveler (Lumine or Aether),an interstellar visitor searching for your lost twin sibling (Lumine or Aether). This is a journey that takes you across seven unique elemental nations, each ruled by an elemental god known as an Archon. With each region you pass by, you will meet different people, that will become your long time friends and adventurures that will help you along your journey. As you journey on with your best travelling companion Paimon, you will be tasting and discovering different types of dishes and specialty dishes(made with each characters special touch) from each region.
