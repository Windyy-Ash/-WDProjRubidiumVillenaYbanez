Guide â€” How to get ingredients

Quick, practical tips for gathering cooking materials and common sources in the world of Teyvat.

  • Local specialties: Each region (Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, etc.) has unique specialty items.
  • Wild ingredients: Mint, meat, and more are often found near roads, fields, and under trees. Explore coastlines, and hills.
  • Markets & vendors: NPC vendors sell ingredients; check different towns (daily restocks).
  • Event rewards: Limited-time events often reward cooking ingredients.

Simple overview â€” What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG where players control a party of characters with elemental abilities. Core elements summarized:

  • Exploration: Traverse large open regions, solve environmental puzzles, gather materials, and discover lore.
  • Combat: Real-time combat combining normal attacks, charged attacks, and elemental skills/bursts. Elemental reactions (e.g., Vaporize, Melt) are central.
  • Party building: You build 4-character teams that synergize via elements and support roles.
  • Progression: Level up characters, ascend them, unlock talents, equip artifacts and weapons, and refine gear.
  • Content types: Archon quests (main story), world quests, domains, bosses, events, and co-op activities.
